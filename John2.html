John Wick Chapter 2 (2017)



John Wick: Chapter 2 is a 2017 American action thriller film directed by Chad Stahelski and written by Derek Kolstad. It is the second installment in the John Wick film series, and the sequel to the 2014 film John Wick. It stars Keanu Reeves as the title character, a former assassin who is forced out of retirement by a former associate who wants to seize control of a shadowy international assassins' guild. The film also features Common, Laurence Fishburne, Riccardo Scamarcio, Ruby Rose, John Leguizamo, and Ian McShane in supporting roles.

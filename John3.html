John Wick Chapter 3 (2019)



John Wick 3 is the third installment of the action thriller film series starring Keanu Reeves as the titular character, a former hitman who is hunted by assassins after breaking the rules of the underworld. The film follows John's journey from New York to Casablanca, where he seeks help from his old friend Sofia (Halle Berry) and tries to find a way to end his exile. Along the way, he faces many enemies, including Zero (Mark Dacascos), a skilled ninja who works for the High Table, the shadowy organization that controls the assassins' guild. The film also features Laurence Fishburne as the Bowery King, Anjelica Huston as the Director, and Ian McShane as Winston, the manager of the New York Continental hotel. The film is directed by Chad Stahelski and written by Derek Kolstad, Shay Hatten, Chris Collins, and Marc Abrams, based on a story by Kolstad. The film was released on May 17, 2019, and received generally positive reviews from critics and audiences. It grossed over $327 million worldwide and became the highest-grossing film in the franchise. A sequel, John Wick: Chapter 4, was released in March 2023.

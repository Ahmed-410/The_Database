John Wick(2014)



John Wick is a 2014 action thriller movie starring Keanu Reeves as the title character, a retired assassin who seeks revenge on the criminals who killed his dog and stole his car. The movie follows John as he tracks down the son of a Russian mob boss, who is the main culprit behind the attack. Along the way, he faces many enemies and allies from his past, as he re-enters the underworld of assassins and hitmen. The movie was well received by critics and audiences, who praised its stylish action sequences, cinematography, and Reeves' performance. It also spawned three sequels and a spin-off series.

Superman III(1983)



Superman 3 was the third installment of Christopher Reeves Superman, in this movie we see Gus Gorman played by Richard Pryor being hired by an industrialist named Ross Webster played by Robert Vaughn to gain control over the weather but Superman sabotages his plans. An annoyed Ross asks Gus to locate Kryptonite to kill Superman but they instead create an alter ego of Superman who is not exactly a good guy

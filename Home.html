Home Alone (1990)



Home Alone is a 1990 comedy film directed by Chris Columbus and written by John Hughes. It stars Macaulay Culkin as Kevin McCallister, an eight-year-old boy who is accidentally left behind by his family when they go on a Christmas vacation to Paris. Kevin has to defend his home from two burglars, Harry and Marv, played by Joe Pesci and Daniel Stern, who try to break in and steal valuables. Along the way, Kevin learns to appreciate his family and makes friends with a lonely neighbor, Old Man Marley, played by Roberts Blossom. The film was a huge hit at the box office, earning over $476 million worldwide. It also received positive reviews from critics and audiences, who praised the humor, the performances, and the heartwarming message. Home Alone is considered a classic Christmas movie and one of the best family comedies of all time.
